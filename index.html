Galaxy Fight Club
The First Cross-IP PVP game for the NFT Universe




Description
Token Sale

HIGHLIGHTS

  • The Galaxy Fight Club is a real-time PvP game bringing together different NFT collections on one single platform.
  • On Galaxy Fight Club, users 'login' and battle each other with their NFT characters

PRODUCT

The first cross-IP PvP game for the NFT Universe


Galaxy Fight Club (GFC) is the first cross-IP, cross-platform (PC & Mobile) PvP fighting game, where holders of different NFT collections can battle with each other to both win and earn rewards through play-to-earn mechanics. It has a fast-paced MOBA gameplay that is mobile-native and similar to Brawl Stars. Each 3v3 match is around 5 minutes long and the team that gets 20 kills first wins the match.

PROBLEM

Siloed NFT collections and lack of Cross-IP elements


IP interoperability is a key attribute to a functional Metaverse & NFTs are designed to be composable Lego bricks for others to build upon. For example, the most popular NFT projects today such as BAYC, Loot adventure gear or more recently Cryptoadz, all have one commonality, which is that they all positioned themselves as a Lego brick to be built upon. BAYC was the first project to give their holders full commercial rights, which allowed a second layer of derivative work to flourish; Loot was the first project at the time to launch just a list of text/words and invite others to give it meaning and interpretation. More recently, Cryptoadz was one of the first projects to give its users public domain licenses to their NFTs. In short, NFTs are designed in a way for other creators and builders to build something on top of them.
When it comes to games like Super Smash Bros, the reason that they are so fun is that characters from different IPs are coming together and players can for example pick Pikachu to fight Mario. It's this cross-IP element that makes these games enjoyable to play over the years. Yet the current NFT landscape is still very siloed. Making use of the interoperability of digital items/assets in a meaningful way is key to creating a long-lasting functioning NFT ecosystem.

SOLUTION

Providing utility for NFT collections


NFT holders are looking for a way to ‘use’ their NFTs. Galaxy Fight Club will fill this gap by being the first cross-IP PvP game platform in the NFT Universe.

BUSINESS MODEL

Sustainable revenue through royalties and NFT drops


GFC will generate revenue through two channels. The first is by taking a 5% royalty from all secondary trades in a similar fashion to the Axie Infinity model. The second channel is through NFT drops. Together these two revenue channels will be enough for GFC to be self-sustaining in the medium to long term. The initial capital raised from the strategic sale and the IDO will help GFC accelerate development and user acquisition through paid advertising. GFC plans to hire more game artists, coders and developers as well as onboard a CMO and COO. The current war chest allows for a runway of around 36 months.

MARKET

Building the metaverse from the bottom-up


There are a few projects that could be considered as competitors to GFC, although GFC is unique in the way that it will be the only game that allows users to log in with their own NFT assets and that is optimized for an exciting battle experience. The three main competitors are Brawl Stars, Thetan Arena and THE SANDBOX. Brawl Stars is not NFT-based and offers no P2E mechanics, which are foundational to building out a functional Metaverse. Thetan arena is a game that is built top-down, while GFC builds a Metaverse from the bottom-up to allow players to use their own NFTs, to which their social identity is linked to. This is why GFC starts from the existing NFT communities and builds a game for these communities instead of building a game first and then releasing NFTs in hope of people buying them. The core attraction to NFTs is the social status and identity it gives them. A successful Metaverse must allow players to keep these intangibles. The Sandbox game is quite different to GFC's game genre, as it creates a Metaverse that is optimized for building and relaxing and is thus fundamentally not designed and optimized for PvP excitement.

By establishing partnerships with tier-1 NFT projects like CyberKongz, Illuvium, Animetas and many others, Galaxy Fight Club is taking advantage of network effects and is the go-to platform for cross-IP battles. GFC is developing a first-of-its-kind cross-chain token contract, which allows the game to run on an L2 for speed and lower transaction fees while optimizing trade volume on NFTs by keeping them on the Ethereum mainnet.

TOKEN UTILITY

$GCOIN: The in-game, deflationary token underpinning the game economy


$GCOIN is designed to be the in-game currency that underpins the entire game economy and game loop. The token is designed to be deflationary even if no trading would occur on the market. This is because there is a fixed supply of 150M $GCOIN, while the demand is driven by in-game utilities, which will increase as the userbase of the game increases.

  • SYN CITY is the first-ever Mafia Metaverse. Here you fight, loot, build and govern through your own syndicate.
  • $SYN is the governance token for the metaverse and core of the Mafia-as-a-DAO system. Users stake $SYN to generate votes to delegate to any syndicate leaders. A limited number of the highest voted leaders will be on the High Table to govern the earning pools of the metaverse.
  • $8 million invested by Angels such as Justin Kan (Co-founder of Twitch), Do Kwon (Co-founder of Terra), Kieran Warwick (Co-founder of Illuvium), Santiago R Santos (Ex-Partner of Parafi Capital), leading VCs including Animoca Brands, Spartan Group and HOF and strategic partners including Overwolf, Merit Circle, YGG, GuildFi and many more.

PRODUCT

Be the boss and take control of turfs, business, loot, and raid other syndicates to expand your empire.


SYN CITY is the first-ever Mafia Metaverse. Here you fight, loot, build and lead. Here you join a syndicate and run businesses, tax your underlings and pay tribute to your bosses through blockchain-backed assets. With enough Ambition, Power, and Swagger you can sit at the head of a syndicate and help chart the course of the Mafia Metaverse.

Displaying a stylized take on the degen and underworld, SYN CITY takes this concept a step further by making the game free to play where you can play and earn. Strategize to build a ruthless empire backed by a gritty syndicate of peers, friends, and enemies alike. The Mafia will allow #synners to grind their way up through daily missions, events, battles, trades, farms, and much more.

PROBLEM

The barriers to entry into the PLAY & EARN model are high and there is limited incentives for the players involved


Most blockchain game economy designs are not working. For starters, it’s not fair to ask users to pay over $1000 before they can even enter and play the game. The result of this is that users tend to earn back their initial cost, they withdraw and sell the assets immediately to recover from the initial investments. This is a death spiral.

SOLUTION

Everyone can be a part of and thrive within the metaverse by bringing the FREE TO PLAY into PLAY TO EARN


SYN CITY is building a mafia metaverse with the following features:
  • Free to play and earn: anyone can come and engage, no ownership of NFT needed
  • Multiple revenue streams: users would have multiple revenue streams by engaging from grinding through events, re-allocating assets to generate returns, and lead your syndicate to earn like a mob through the MaaD system.
  • Focus on the mafia vertical, making it easy for people to click and engage and easy to form a consensus.

BUSINESS MODEL

Be the leader you have always wanted to be by creating, taking charge, and leading a group of degenerate syndicate members. Raid loot, layout strategies, expand your crews, and collect tax.


The Mafia-as-a-DAO (MaaD) consists of leaders on the High Table that manage the governance committee. Users are incentivized and SYN CITY revenue is generated by the MaaD system.
  • Players stake $SYN to generate $sPOWER and delegate that to the syndicate leaders. There are unlimited syndicates with unlimited leaders, but only top 7 leaders with the most $sPOWER will be allowed to join the High Table to manage the governance committee which shapes the earnings events and allocate the earnings pool.
  • Syndicate leaders could offer benefits in assets, special events, and many others to the members of their syndicates for recruitment, engagement and loyalty.
  • Syndicate leaders can tax their members for the benefits offered. The SYN CITY then platform charges a tax to the syndicates, out of which a portion goes to the overall community as Universal Basic Income (UBI), rest goes to the developers.

MARKET

This is the crypto era and everyone should have an equal right to be able to play and earn. In the Mafia Metaverse, it should be FREE TO EARN.


It’s not fair to ask people to pay $1000 hard-earned money before they can play or earn from the game. The Free to PLAY to EARN model ensures there is a global market base with a large funnel. And by building for both mobile and desktop, SYN CITY is ready for mass adoption.
